OUR FOUNDER

Dr. Lin Song, (etc.)

Dr. Lin Song has extensive experience in research and development in multi-disciplinary projects over his 14 years career. He has worked in projects like electric vehicle, edible capsule robot, mobile robots, intelligent actuators, etc., and, as an inventor of 10 patents and author of 13 scientific papers, he was awarded the prestigious Singapore National Technology Award in 2006.

More encouragingly, most of his patents were purchased by a MNC company later on and the technologies he developed have been licensed to many SMC and MNC companies. One recent R&D results from the team led by him even facilitated a new business category for the company.
