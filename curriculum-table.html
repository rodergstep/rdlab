WHY OUR CURRICULUM IS DIFFERENT?

STEM is a concept grounded first and foremost in problem solving from a multi-faceted and inter-disciplinary perspective.

In our opinion, it should neither just cater to the science, technology, engineering, or math aspects of a student’s education, nor be the pursuit of professional knowledge of each individual subject. Instead, it should be the promotion of knowledge mastery through a comprehensive and synergistic study of different STEM subjects.

A well designed STEM-based approach to education includes the integration of all subject matter that may be brought to tackle everyday problems. Therefore, unlike most of the STEM programs currently available in the market, our S-STEM programs are dedicated to providing fun and intellectually stimulating experiences by providing a synergistic and cross- disciplinary curriculum.Such a curriculum will help students to cultivate scientific thinking skills, develop problem-solving capabilities, and improve their creativities.
