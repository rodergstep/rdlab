GENERAL INFO ABOUT THE CLUB

Engineering For Kids - STEM Club is open Monday – Thursday from 5:30pm – 7:30pm. STEM Club is designed with education and fun in mind. Everyday Engineering concepts will be presented, discussed, and key concepts taught by our professionally trained staff. Students will then be presented with a challenge related to the concept and utilize the Engineering Design Process, to complete the challenge of the day.

All our classes are designed with experience level in mind, beginning students will be taught more of a step-by-step process, and as their engineering experience increases students will be asked to come up with their own builds and designs based on the experience they have gained through previous club lessons. Monthly calendars will be posted on the website and will be available on site at the club. Check back often for updates.

  • Benefits of the Club

  • Exposure to Robotics, Coding, 3D printing, and General Engineering Concepts

  • Hands-On Experience

  • Curriculum that challenges students in a positive way

  • Problem-Solving

  • Critical Thinking

  • Peer Collaboration

  • Fun and Engaging Environment

  • Real-World problem-solving skills

  • Positive Experiences with Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathmatics
